Fruit flavored water

Ingredients

  • 5 cups of water
  • 1 cup ice cubes(Optional)
  • 1/2 cup small strawberries
  • 1 lime, sliced

Steps

  1. Pour water into a pitcher. Add ice and strawberries. Squeeze lime slices to release some of their juice into the water before adding them in. Stir to combine flavors.
